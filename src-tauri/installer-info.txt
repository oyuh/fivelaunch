FiveLaunch — FiveM profile launcher
by Lawson Hart · fivelaunch.help

WHAT IT DOES
FiveLaunch lets you keep multiple FiveM "clients" (profiles), each with its own
mods, plugins, citizen data, and GTA V graphics settings. When you launch a
client, FiveLaunch swaps those files into your FiveM install for you, then puts
everything back the way it was after you play.

BEFORE YOU START — BACK UP YOUR DATA
The first time you link a client, FiveLaunch moves your existing FiveM files
aside (into a managed backups folder). Please make sure you have a copy of your
current FiveM.app mods/plugins/citizen folders, your GTA V settings.xml, and your
CitizenFX.ini before linking. You'll confirm this inside the app on first run.

Installs per-user (no admin required) and updates itself automatically from
GitHub releases.
